HVAC Maintenance Tips

Check Furnace Filter

Replace your furnace filter every 1–3 months.

Check Thermostat Battery

Low batteries can stop your HVAC from running.

Check Breaker

Make sure your furnace or AC breaker hasn't tripped.

Clear Exhaust Vents

Ensure snow or debris is not blocking exhaust vents.
